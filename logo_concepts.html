QuizMaster Brand Identity

Proposed Logo Concepts by FalitoNGL

The "Electric Q"

A stylized 'Q' pierced by a lightning bolt. Represents speed, power, and instant knowledge.

The "Glass Brain"

A transparent, glowing brain network. Symbolizes intelligence, neural connections, and premium design.

The "Cyber Shield"

A digital shield protecting the core. Represents security, defense, and robust knowledge.

The "Neural Q"

Abstract Q formed by connected nodes. Symbolizes deep learning and AI-powered logic.

The "HexaGuard"

Cyber-security focus. Structurally sound, defended, and precise.

The "Pulse Mind"

Brain shape with an active EKG heartbeat. Represents active thinking and quick reactions.

The "Pixel Trophy"

Retro gaming aesthetic. Emphasizes the gamification and 'fun' aspect of the quiz.
